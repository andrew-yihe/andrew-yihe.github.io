Zumba GO

A Training Platform Application for Zumba Dancing

2021

About

The purpose of this project is to investigate current limitations of Zumba leaning and design a novel application operated in dual-screen devices that can assist users in training Zumba - specifically in guiding users’ dancing movements and tracking activity outcome.

Secondary research and interviews with Zumba learners were conducted. The main findings are: difficult in finding body coordination for learners with no dance background, hard to measure the intensity of movement, lack of standard of movement, and unable to keep up the fast-paced music rhythm.

So how might we allow Zumba learners to monitor activity so that they can improve the quality of movement? I used 10+10 sketching method to determine the best solution - An app that compares user’s movement with instructor’s pre-recorded one using additional fitness tracker and analyses them on the main device.

50+ sketches were created throughout the project. I have developed a great UX practice of using sketching methods in idea forming phase and have gained the ability to demonstrate a wide variety of ideas for potential solutions.

This is an individual project.
