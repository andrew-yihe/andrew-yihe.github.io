The Harrods Hive

UI/UX Design for Harrods Event in Shanghai Fashion Week 2021

2021

About

The Harrods Hive is a UI/UX design project I have created at Farfetch. As part of Shanghai Fashion Week, The Harrods Hive is a three days events hosted by Harrods to connect designers and the fashion talents with industry mentors. The app, which can be accessed via WeChat mini programs, provides an overview of the event as well as detailed information of each sessions. My responsibilities for the project were to design the visual concept and layout of the user interface, create visual components, and produce prototypes of user interactions.

To incorporate the concept of “hive” into the visuals, I applied the hexagon as the main shape of the components in the home page as navigation buttons for user to explore. The shape was also used in the background for an additional sense of space and depth. Since the interaction flow is minimal and straightforward, I experimented with different interaction patterns in terms of page transitions and produced motion mockups to present to our clients.

This project is my first industrial experience of creating main visuals from scratch, starting with concept researching and brainstorming. I gained insights into the entire process of initiating a new project and followed along with it throughout the journey.

This is one of the commercial projects I worked on during my internship at Farfetch.
