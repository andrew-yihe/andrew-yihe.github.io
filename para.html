VR Training System Using Digital Twin Technology

2021

View on Behance

About

In collaboration with Telstra Australia, Paramedic training+ is a virtual reality system prototype designed to help paramedics to perform engaging contextual training. Applying digital twins of real cases, the system aims to minimise the gap between the training contents and the real-world scenarios.

Based on the assumption that “the lack of continual up-to-date training decreases paramedics’ response readiness for duty”, I conducted semi-structured interviews with full-time paramedics as well as secondary research on academic paper and news to identify limitations, and coded data into key findings and created personas to understand user needs.

The user experience of the system was defined through information architecture, wireframes and user flows. The high-fidelity prototype consists of two main parts: the tablet application UI that is for managing all the training related data, and a sample VR training environment.
